Tweetsichord

music from hashtags

Tweetsichord searches Twitter for the hashtag or username you provide and determines what note to play based on the characters it finds in those tweets. It can also search for multiple tags and play them together. The end result is music generated by the social activity going on at Twitter.

Tweetsichord is still under development. Here are some features to expect soon -

  • full support for Firefox
  • an instrument selection for each tag
  • some visulization
